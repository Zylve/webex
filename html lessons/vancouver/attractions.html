Attractions and Points of Interest

Vancouver is home to many places to explore and fun things to do.

Just a few minutes north of the city, we have the North Shore mountains. Here you can find unlimited hiking and mountain biking trails, along with a few ski resorts.

Within the core of Vancouver, there are many places to visit. Downtown offers many museums and walking experiences for you to enjoy.
